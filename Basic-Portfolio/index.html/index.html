About Me

My name is Daija Cozier Jones. I reside in Rose Park, UT with my mom and two brothers. In my 18 years on this glorious cluster of elements we call Earth I have spent at least 15 questioning everday functions in an attempt to understand our world. Of those 15 years, I have spent 12 creating in an attempt to understand and reflect on our world. I am hopeful that coding will become another form of my Earthly expression.
